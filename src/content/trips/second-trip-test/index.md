A motorcycle (motorbike, bike, or, if three-wheeled, a trike) is a two or three-wheeled [motor vehicle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motor_vehicle) [steered](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steering) by a [handlebar](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motorcycle_handlebar) from a saddle-style seat.[\[1]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motorcycle#cite_note-definitions-1)[\[2]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motorcycle#cite_note-2)[\[3]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motorcycle#cite_note-3)

Motorcycle designs vary greatly to suit a range of different purposes: [long-distance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Long-distance_motorcycle_riding) travel, [commuting](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motorcycle_commuting), [cruising](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cruising_(driving)), [sport](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motorcycle_sport) (including [racing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motorcycle_racing)), and [off-road](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Off-roading) riding. [Motorcycling](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motorcycling) is riding a motorcycle and being involved in other related social activities such as joining a [motorcycle club](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motorcycle_club) and attending [motorcycle rallies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Motorcycle_rally).
